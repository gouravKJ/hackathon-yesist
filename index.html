Solar Energy

Solar panels convert sunlight directly into electricity through photovoltaic cells. They're ideal for homes with good sun exposure and unshaded roofs.

High Potential in Sunny Regions

Benefits of Solar Energy:

  • Zero emissions during operation
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • 25+ year lifespan for quality panels
  • Energy independence and reduced utility bills

Wind Energy

Wind turbines convert kinetic energy from wind into electricity. Small-scale turbines can work well in rural areas with consistent wind patterns.

Medium Potential for Most Locations

Benefits of Wind Energy:

  • Highly efficient in windy locations
  • Can produce power day and night
  • Small footprint with large power generation
  • Low operating costs after installation
  • 20+ year lifespan for quality turbines

Considerations:

  • Initial installation costs Rs. 4.5 to 6.5 crores per MW
  • Requires consistent wind speeds (ideally 12+ mph)
  • Higher initial costs for small-scale systems
  • Potential noise concerns and visual impact
  • Local zoning restrictions may apply
  • Regular maintenance required

Hydro Energy

Micro-hydropower systems use flowing water to generate electricity. Ideal for properties with consistent water flow from streams or rivers.

Location-Specific Potential

Benefits of Hydro Energy:

  • Consistent and reliable power generation
  • 24/7 electricity production potential
  • Long system lifespan (50+ years)
  • Low maintenance once installed
  • Minimal environmental impact for micro systems

Considerations:

  • Initial installation costs Rs 70 million to Rs 200 million per MW
  • Requires access to flowing water year-round
  • Environmental permits often needed
  • Higher initial installation complexity
  • Seasonal water flow variations
  • Limited to specific geographical locations

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal heat pumps use the earth's constant temperature to heat and cool homes efficiently. Works in almost any location with sufficient land for installation.

Widely Applicable

Benefits of Geothermal Energy:

  • Highly efficient heating and cooling
  • Significant energy savings (30-70%)
  • Works in virtually any climate
  • Very long system lifespan (25-50 years)
  • Quiet operation and invisible infrastructure
